About EIRA

Professional Background

A Decade of Executive Level Support

I help founders, executives, and investors optimize their most scarce resource: time.

Before establishing my independent executive assistant practice, I spent over eight years working in-house with venture capital firms and high-growth technology startups in New York and London. I've supported managing directors, serial founders, and high-net-worth individuals, mastering the art of high-pressure operations, rapid prioritization, and calendar engineering.

My approach is built on proactivity. I do not wait to receive instructions; I analyze operational pipelines, identify upcoming conflicts, and deploy solutions independently. This allows the leaders I support to exit the weeds of daily admin and remain focused purely on macro-strategic decisions.
